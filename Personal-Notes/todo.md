## Conrad's Silver Forging Priority (90 Ingot Budget)

Since Conrad is already swinging **Frostner**, the priority shifts from offense to survival and specialized defense in the Mountains.

---

### 1. Top Priority: Wolf Armor Chest (20 Silver)
* **Level:** 1
* **The Reason:** This provides permanent frost resistance. It is the most important "quality of life" upgrade in the Mountains, freeing up your potion slots for Healing or Stamina meads.
* **Stop at Level 1:** The base armor jump is the goal; don't sink more silver into levels yet.

### 2. Shield: Silver Shield (15 Silver)
* **Level:** 2 or 3 (Total ~30 Silver)
* **The Reason:** For a player who relies on the **Shield Parry** technique, this is essential. A Level 3 Silver Shield can parry a 2-star Wolf or a Fenring.
* **Investment:** Spend the extra silver to reach Level 3. It is your primary insurance policy against high-damage enemies.

### 3. The Weapon: Upgrade Frostner (15 Silver)
* **Level:** 2 (Upgrade Only)
* **The Reason:** You already have the base weapon. Upgrading to Level 2 increases blunt, frost, and spirit damage, making it much more effective against Stone Golems and the undead.

### 4. Utility: Drake Helmet (20 Silver)
* **Level:** 1
* **The Reason:** This provides the highest armor rating for the head slot in this tier. (Requires 2 Drake Trophies).
